11 Value-Up Theses

Four levers that could lift returns if the investigation clears — none is fully underwritable pre-DD
Important but DD-dependent

Balance-sheet optimisation

Deploy the ~¥4.1bn net cash and monetise investment securities. Excess cash reduces headline EV but does not make this a classic leverage-driven LBO — sponsor equity stays high at ¥7.7bn because debt capacity is constrained. Subject to legal, operating, lender and investigation DD.

High control, phased

Margin expansion

Rationalise group overhead and lift the software mix; turn around or partner the loss-making Mobility unit to move blended operating margin from ~8% toward 12%+.

Higher risk, higher reward

Strategic re-rating of optionality

Professionalise and scale the HD-map / Level-4 franchise into a separately valuable asset, supporting an exit to an auto OEM, map consolidator or geospatial buyer at a premium multiple.

Downside protection

Defensive compounding

Protect and grow the recurring public-sector software base through i-Construction and national 3D-data tailwinds — the floor under every scenario.

13 Gating Item — Subsidiary Probe

An unresolved investigation at a 100%-owned subsidiary overrides the investment case until cleared
What happened — Akisoku
  • Jan 2024
    AISAN acquires 100% of Akisoku (有限会社秋測), an Akita-based survey-instrument sales / repair firm.
  • 11 Mar 2026
    A supplier queries unpaid debt at the subsidiary's marketing centre (Ueda, Nagano) — the trigger.
  • Internal review
    Suspected improper transactions by a subsidiary director; forgery & concealment of documents.
  • Widening
    Further forged documents and concealment of inventory losses / unpaid debts identified.
  • 3 Apr 2026
    Special Investigation Committee established (external lawyer + CPAs, outside directors / auditors).
  • Market
    Shares fell to ~¥1,643 intraday on disclosure and drifted to ~¥1,490s by May; FY26 impact undetermined.
GATE — required before any bid
  • Final investigation report & quantified financial impact
  • Restated / audited financial statements
  • Evidence the issues are contained to Akisoku
  • Remediation of group-wide internal controls
